Scrimmage at Dartmouth High School (November 19, 2016)

Overall, our scrimmage in Dartmouth went very well. Our robot performed approximately how we expected, and we won all 5 of our matches. We were able to hit the cap ball and score in autonomous (see the video!), push the beacons, and score 8-10 particles into the corner vortex per match. This was a very positive beginning to the season for us and we hope to continue in this manner. However, there are still many improvements to make for our Qualifier in December, so it's time to get working!
