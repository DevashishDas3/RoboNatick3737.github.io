Katie Tumang (Administrative Leader)

Katie Tumang is 18 years old and is in her senior year at Natick High School. She joined RoboNatick as a freshman and has remained in the shop ever since. She became a financial leader in sophomore year. This position was later renamed to the administrative leader, and Katie has been the administrative leader of her team for the past two years. In the shop, she enjoys the sense of community shared by all three RoboNatick teams. Although the three teams are separate and occasionally compete against one another, they are constantly trading advice on the subjects of robots, classes, and the best ways to cut out a cardboard pizza plate. Outside of robotics, Katie enjoys drawing and digital art, and practices karate. She plans to study cellular and molecular biology in college, and ultimately research genetic disorders.
